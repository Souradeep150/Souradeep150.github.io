Souradeep Mukhopadhyay

I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Arizona State University (QS World University Rankings 2026: #=173), advised by Prof. Chitta Baral. My research interests include LLM-based agentic systems, systematic LLM failure modes, and world models.

I received my B.Tech in Computer Science with First Class Honors and distinction from Jadavpur University (QS WUR 2026: #=676; NIRF 2025: #9 in University; #1 in State Public Universities), where I was advised by Prof. Ram Sarkar. I then completed my M.Tech. in Artificial Intelligence at the Indian Institute of Science, Bengaluru (QS WUR 2026: #=219; NIRF 2025: #1 in University), and joined Nokia as a Research Specialist for one year.
